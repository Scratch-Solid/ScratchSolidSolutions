We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption of data at rest (AES-256)
- Secure password storage (bcrypt hashing)
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication

**Organizational Safeguards:**
- Employee training on data privacy
- Limited access to personal information
- Confidentiality agreements
- Security incident response procedures

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

You can request access to your personal data by:

### Right to Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data:

You can object to certain uses of your data, particularly for marketing purposes.

You can request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.

### Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time, where applicable.
